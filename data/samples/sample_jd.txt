Job Title: Junior AI/ML Engineer (Intern)

Role Overview:
We are looking for a skilled AI/ML Intern to contribute to our core data science initiatives. You will be responsible for developing end-to-end ML pipelines, training Deep Learning models, and refining predictive systems to enhance our data-driven decision-making.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop, validate, and tune machine learning models for classification and regression tasks.  
- Design and implement end-to-end ML pipelines, including data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model optimization.  
- Architect and train Deep Learning architectures (CNNs) for complex image or dataset analysis.  
- Collaborate with the development team to integrate backend solutions with data-driven models. 

Required Technical Skills:
- Programming: Strong proficiency in Python.  
- AI/ML Frameworks: Practical expertise with TensorFlow, Keras, and scikit-learn.  
- Data Science: Strong knowledge of NumPy, pandas, and Matplotlib for EDA, feature engineering, and hyperparameter tuning.  
- Tools: Proficient in using Git/GitHub for version control and Jupyter Notebook/VS Code for development. 

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with computer vision architectures (CNNs) and hyperspectral or multispectral data.  
- Familiarity with SQL for database operations.  Basic understanding of PyTorch and cloud-based deployments.  
- Experience with backend integration and API management (e.g., Fast API, Supabase).
